My Brothers Keeper

2017-05-31T13:08:09 · Robert

Creativity comes in many forms. Today, I would like to display another form of creative expression I have been interested in with a Sons of Anarchy fanfiction story. I've wrote fiction for as long as I can remember, and a couple of years ago I started working on a Sons of Anarchy fanfiction about life after the series finale.

Why Sons of Anarchy?

The Mrs and I were big fans of the show when it was on the air and I always thought it would be fun to try my hand writing a story like this. Kurt Sutter did an amazing job crafting the show from beginning to end, so I hope that my first stab at fanfiction is at least on par with the twists and turns that Kurt put into the original SOA. The story will be a continuation from the series finale and will not be written in an alternative universe where the main characters are living completely different lives. This story is how I feel the family would have continued on after the show ended. A couple of years have actually gone by since I wrote the prologue and first two chapters. I'm publishing these in an attempt to get me to finish this story before NaNoWriMo, when I plan on writing a full novel. It's also a good way for me to test out the Chapters for Authors plugin I built and released a few months ago.

Chapters for Authors

Now that I've had the chance to use the plugin, adding in my first book and a couple chapters I have the ability to see what needs updated in the plugin. There's a few things I need to update, like the addition of a widget and shortcode to display a list of books, chapters, etc. I've also got a few other ideas on how to make the plugin more robust, which is being actively worked on. The release for this will be coming soon, I'm working on a few other things over at WP Dispensary first. If you use the Chapters for Authors plugin and have any ideas on how to make it better, let me know!
