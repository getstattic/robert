WordPress® Plugin Updates

Important Update on Plugin Updates

Due to recent developments initiated by the leadership at WordPress®, access to the plugin repository has been restricted for certain hosting providers and developers, including WP Engine users.

This action has affected the ability of users to automatically update their plugins through the WordPress.org repository.

What Happened?

WordPress® has begun limiting access to its repository for certain developers and hosting providers due to an ongoing conflict between Matthew Mullenweg, WP Engine and the greater WordPress Community.

As a result, plugin updates that typically happen seamlessly through your WordPress® dashboard may no longer be available automatically.

This restriction was imposed by Matthew directly and it impacts plugin functionality, security, and overall site performance if updates are missed.

Why This Matters

Automatic plugin updates are essential for maintaining security and ensuring compatibility with new versions of WordPress®.

The recent restrictions pose a potential risk as users may miss critical updates, leaving sites vulnerable to security threats and bugs.

What I’m Doing About It

I’m aware of how crucial timely updates are to the success and security of your website.

I am actively working on transitioning all of my plugins to a new update system that will no longer rely on WordPress.org.

This new system will allow for direct updates from trusted sources, ensuring continued support and security.

A More Reliable Update System

Moving forward, I am developing an independent update mechanism to ensure that you:

  • Receive updates directly, without depending on the WordPress.org repository.
  • Stay informed about important updates and fixes in real-time.
  • Maintain the same level of security and functionality you expect from my plugins.

What You Can Do Now

While this transition is ongoing, please follow the steps below to ensure you continue receiving updates:

  1. Visit robertdevore.com for the latest updates and instructions.
  2. Manually check for updates until the new update system is fully in place.
  3. Stay tuned for upcoming releases that will streamline the process for future updates.

I appreciate your patience and understanding during this time, and I’m committed to delivering a solution that’s even more reliable than before.

Next Steps

I will publish a new blog post as soon as the new update system is live. In the meantime, please don’t hesitate to reach out if you have any questions or concerns.

Thank you for your continued trust and support!
